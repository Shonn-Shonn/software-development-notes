Project Risks
->Anything that can go wrong(endanger project success)

Types of Risks
.technology
.organization
.requirements
.people
.tools


Dealing with Risks
.avoid(replan or change requirements)
.confine(restrict the scope of its effect)
.mitigate(devise tests to see if it occurs)
.monitor(constantly be on the lookout of it)


Risk Analysis
-Likelihood(the chance that is going to happen)
Apply 80:20 rule
-> 80% of all projects risks is accounted by 20% of identified risks
